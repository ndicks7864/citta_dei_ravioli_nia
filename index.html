About Us

Citta Dei Ravioli is your top-notch Italian restaurant located here in North Carolina. Known for our famous variety of ravioli. Our top sellers include Spinach and Manchego Cheese Ravioli, Classic Three Cheese Ravioli and our raviolis with a slight twist, Seafood Shrimp Twist Ravioli and Butternut Squash Ravioli. All of our pasta is made here by the best chefs from around the states. Made from scratch here in our own kitchen, we receive freshly imported ingredients to use in our dishes. Assuring you that our food is the best quality it can be. Not a big fan of ravioli? There is no need to worry, we serve other options such as our popular Zuppa Toscana Soup and authentic Greek Salad. Want to experience real Italian? Come to Città Dei Ravioli where every meal is made with the magical touch of Italian cuisine.
